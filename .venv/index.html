Sneaker Authentication

Verify the authenticity of your sneakers with our advanced AI-based technology.

How It Works

Our advanced AI-based technology analyzes your uploaded sneaker image and compares it to a database of authentic sneakers. The system checks for various factors such as design, materials, stitching, and logos. After analyzing the image, it provides you with detailed information on the authenticity of the sneaker, including the reasons why it's deemed genuine or fake.
